Logic Game

You open the book and find the following information:

There are four people involved in this:

  1. Alice
  2. Bob
  3. Charlie
  4. Dave
Each one has a role at the company, and they all have their own project. The roles are:
  1. Developer
  2. Ceo
  3. Intern
  4. Project Manager
And the projects are:
  1. Design a mobile App
  2. Send an Email
  3. Debug a Piece of Code
  4. Deploy a Webpage

Clues:

  1. The CEO is neither Bob nor Charlie.
  2. The four people are Alice, the Intern, the one Debugging, and the one writing the Email.
  3. Of the Developer and the Project Manager, one is Dave, and one is Debugging.
  4. The person designing the App is either Alice or Dave.
  5. Bob is not writing the Email.
  6. Charlie is the developer.
  7. Of the person writing the Email and Alice, one is the CEO, and one is the Project Manager.
  8. The person deploying the Webpage is the Intern.
